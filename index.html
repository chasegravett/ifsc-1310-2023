Thoughts on Web Accessibilty

Derek Featherstone lecture over Web accessibility just made me realize how important it is to have access to the web despite your situation. I worked in a long term care facility and I got the opportunity to to witness first hand on this topic. I watched residents that were quadriplegic who were stuck in the bed and despite that use the internet due to different software and hardware. One was scrolling his ipad and moving it with just his eyes and before that he had a mouse that he used his chin to operate on what he wanted to click. The other one used a tube that he blew in to help operate his laptown.
